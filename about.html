About Legatus

What is Legatus MUN?

Legatus Model United Nations is a two-day international United Nations simulation for high school students which, in light of the pandemic, is to be held on video-conferencing platform Zoom. At Legatus MUN, delegates shall gain awareness on the dynamics of the United Nations by assuming the roles of world leaders and international decision makers.

It is an exciting opportunity for young leaders to debate the most pressing issues of the day and to draft innovative solutions to world issues. Participants will develop several skills throughout this process, including leadership skills, public speaking, negotiation, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

Legatus seeks to establish a constructive assembly for open dialogue on a variety of complex issues, including international peace, human rights and socio-economic progress. We ask delegates to think critically about the world that surrounds them and to think in new and ambitious ways. Delegates leave our conference with a new-found passion for diplomacy and greater awareness of the problems that prevail in our world. With the first conference being a massive success with hundreds of delegates, there is no doubt that Legatus has made a longlasting impact on online Model UN.

Why attend Legatus MUN?

The staff of Legatus MUN has been carefully selected to include the most passionate and dedicated dias’. Each background guide is carefully crafted for its respective committee and reflects weeks of thorough research and critical thinking. All of our chairs are committed to Legatus MUN’s ideals, resulting in well-run and focused committees. 

Here, you shall have the opportunity to meet and interact with many students from high schools around the world. We challenge this diverse pool of young leaders to tackle some of the most pressing issues in the world, through sustained discussion, respectful debate, and negotiation. Our conference is uniquely designed to foster negotiation and public speaking skills, introduce students to a range of global challenges, and provide culturally diverse perspectives on a variety of issues.

Who are the organizers of Legatus MUN?

Legatus Model UN is organized by a group of high-school MUN veterans and is facilitated with a highly trained and experienced Executive Board. The founders of Legatus are Akshit Anchan, Anushka Chakraborty and Muhammad Zaid. Feel free to get in touch with them!
